A paragraph is a unit of writing that consists of one or more sentences that relate to a single topic. Paragraphs are essential for organizing long pieces of writing, such as novels, papers, or emails.A paragraph usually begins on a new, indented line. The main idea of a paragraph is usually introduced in the first sentence, called the topic sentence. The idea is then developed further through the sentences that follow.
